# Dar Mansour — Backlink Outreach Plan

Living document for building editorial backlinks to darmansour.com. Backlinks
(other reputable sites linking to us) are one of the biggest levers for climbing
Google rankings, especially for a young site.

**Golden rules**
- Always personalise the first line (quote a real detail from their article) — it doubles reply rate.
- Offering a dinner/tasting is the strongest asset: a blogger who dines almost always writes a linked feature.
- Have high-res, frame-free food photos ready to send.
- Always disclose we are the restaurant (transparency = trust with editors and AI engines).
- Track outcomes in the status table at the bottom.
