Wine Pairing Experience

Crafted to enhance every bite — on the spiced path of Morocco

Koh Phangan's only dedicated Moroccan wine pairing list — natural, characterful labels chosen to elevate the soul of our slow food.

At Dar Mansour, wine is more than a drink — it's a dialogue with the dish. Each label in our curated list is selected to elevate the complexity, warmth and aromatic depth of Moroccan slow food.

Start with the Unexpected
Maynard's Fine Tawny Porto — 2019 — Portugal · Glass (10cl) 290 · Bottle 2300

Pairs with: Berber's Lamb Tajine (prunes & almonds)

Smooth, amber and rich — a silk-sweet, surprising match that enhances the spices and deep savoury flavours of this unique tajine. “Unexpected? Yes. Unforgettable? Absolutely.”

Red Wines
LIGNAC Comte Tolosan IGP — 2021
France · House · Glass (12cl) 200 · Bottle 1200

Grapes: Syrah  ·  Pairs with: Rabat & Chefchaouen's Tajine, Couscous Darna

Bold and peppery Syrah with firm tannins, low acidity and full-bodied finesse.

POGGIO ALTO Negroamaro Puglia IGT — 2020
Italy · 1350

Grapes: Negroamaro  ·  Pairs with: Rabat & Berber's Tajine, Couscous

Deep ruby hue with fruity, subtly spiced aromas. Smooth, round and balanced with soft tannins.

CELLIERS DES PRINCES Sceau du Prince, Côtes-du-Rhône AOP — 2022
France · 1500

Grapes: Grenache, Syrah, Mourvèdre  ·  Pairs with: Rabat & Berber's Tajine, Couscous, Tanjia Marrakchia

Ruby red with purple glints, bursting with red fruits, light spice and silky tannins. Smooth, supple and fruit-forward.

BURGO VIEJO Rioja Crianza DOC — 2019
Spain · 1650

Grapes: Tempranillo  ·  Pairs with: Rabat & Berber's Tajine, Tanjia Marrakchia

Ruby red with black fruits and vanilla notes. Powerful, complex and silky on the palate.

CELLIERS DES PRINCES Blason du Prince, Châteauneuf-du-Pape AOC — 2022
France · 3700

Grapes: Grenache, Syrah, Mourvèdre, Cinsault  ·  Pairs with: Rabat & Berber's Tajine, Tanjia Marrakchia

Ruby red with red and black berries, hints of lavender, thyme and rosemary. Balanced palate with elegant, silky tannins.

White Wines
PUNTI FERRER Limited Edition, Rapel Valley — 2021
Chile · House · Glass (12cl) 200 · Bottle 1200

Grapes: Sauvignon Blanc  ·  Pairs with: Fez, Oualidia & Chefchaouen's Tajine

Pale yellow with green reflections. Tropical fruit, citrus and mineral notes. Fresh, light-bodied with a crisp, zesty finish.

LE PETIT BALTHAZAR Pays d'Oc IGP — 2024
France · 1550

Grapes: Viognier, Sauvignon Blanc  ·  Pairs with: Fez, Oualidia & Chefchaouen's Tajine, Couscous

Pale yellow with silver-green tints. Intense lime, grapefruit and exotic fruit. Crisp and fresh with a fruity finish.

GRANGE MAZAN Côtes de Gascogne IGP — 2022
France · 1600

Grapes: Colombard, Sauvignon Blanc  ·  Pairs with: Fez, Oualidia & Chefchaouen's Tajine

Pale gold with vibrant lime, grapefruit and lychee. Refreshing and juicy with well-balanced acidity.

CHÂTEAU DU JAUNAY Muscadet de Sèvre & Maine sur Lie AOP, Vieilles Vignes — 2023
France · 1650

Grapes: Melon de Bourgogne  ·  Pairs with: Fez, Oualidia & Chefchaouen's Tajine

Shiny gold, crisp citrus and white-flower aromas with mineral hints. Smooth, elegant finish.

Rosé Wines

Grapes: Merlot  ·  Pairs with: Fez & Chefchaouen's Tajine, Couscous
