Below, our full picks by style and by village.

Breakfast in Koh Phangan: More Than the First Meal of the Day

Breakfast on Koh Phangan is part of the island's lifestyle. Some mornings begin with sunrise on the beach, others with yoga, a swim or a few productive hours behind a laptop. Whatever brings you here, there's always a good place nearby to start the day with quality coffee and honest food.

From specialty coffee and artisan bakeries to smoothie bowls, vegan brunches and generous all-day breakfasts, Koh Phangan has one of the most varied morning scenes in southern Thailand. Staying in Sri Thanu, Hin Kong, Thong Sala, Chaloklum or Baan Tai, you'll find cafés that have become favourites with residents, digital nomads and returning travellers.

This guide brings together our favourite breakfast and brunch spots across the island — from relaxed beachfront cafés to neighbourhood roasters serving some of the best coffee and morning food on Koh Phangan. For lunch and dinner, it pairs with our full guide to the best restaurants in Koh Phangan.

Why You Can Trust This Guide

This guide is published by Dar Mansour, a Moroccan restaurant on the road between Hin Kong and Sri Thanu. Breakfast isn't our own service, which makes this an easy list to keep honest: none of these picks are us. They come from living and eating here year-round — the cafés we actually return to for coffee, brunch and a slow morning. Because we're based here, we keep the guide updated through the year as places change and new ones open.

Best Breakfast & Brunch in Koh Phangan

The cafés we come back to when we want a proper breakfast or a slow, unhurried brunch.

Bubba's Coffee

One of the island's best-known coffee names, and a dependable all-rounder for a full brunch. Carefully sourced beans meet generous breakfast plates, smoothie bowls and baked goods, in an easy, welcoming room. With cafés in Baan Tai and Thong Sala and the roastery in Haad Yao, Bubba's has become a morning fixture across the island.

Why we love it — Specialty coffee and a generous brunch, from one of the island's favourite café names.
Best for — Brunch · Specialty coffee · Digital nomads
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Baan Tai (also in Thong Sala; roastery in Haad Yao). View on map ↗

Foxxy's Restaurant & Café

One of Sri Thanu's most dependable all-day cafés, good for a slow breakfast, a relaxed lunch or just a proper coffee. The menu pulls together generous brunch plates, smoothie bowls, fresh salads and homemade cakes with quality specialty coffee, in a bright, welcoming room.

Why we love it — Good coffee, generous brunches and one of Sri Thanu's warmest cafés.
Best for — Breakfast · Brunch · Digital nomads
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — On the main road in Sri Thanu, close to the village's cafés and wellness centres. View on map ↗

SAGE Breakfast & More

Long breakfasts are close to a way of life here, and SAGE leans right into them. Overlooking the sea in Ban Tai, this bright café serves one of the island's fullest all-day breakfast and brunch menus — specialty coffee, smoothie bowls, pancakes, generous egg dishes and lighter options through the day. Vegan, vegetarian or classic, it's covered.

Why we love it — One of the island's best all-day breakfasts, with good coffee and a sea view.
Best for — Breakfast · Brunch · Sea views
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — On the Ban Tai beachfront, overlooking the sea, with easy parking. View on map ↗

Mimi's Café

In the heart of Sri Thanu, Mimi's is a neighbourhood café where residents, remote workers and travellers gather over good Italian coffee, fresh breakfasts and homemade lunches. The kitchen leans on organic eggs and seasonal produce where it can, in a relaxed, friendly room — a natural stop between the beach and a yoga class.

Why we love it — Good Italian coffee and homemade food in one of the village's friendliest cafés.
Best for — Breakfast bowls · Specialty coffee · Digital nomads
Price — ฿
Getting there — In the heart of Sri Thanu village. View on map ↗

Best Specialty Coffee in Koh Phangan

For travellers who care as much about the coffee as the food.

Indigo Specialty Coffee & Bakery

The kind of café that becomes routine after one visit. Indigo has built its name on specialty coffee, pastries baked fresh each morning and an easy welcome — a flat white, a flaky croissant or a slow brunch, all done with a steady hand. Locals, remote workers and travellers all end up here.

Why we love it — Specialty coffee and one of the island's best artisan bakeries.
Best for — Specialty coffee · Breakfast · Fresh pastries
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — On the main road between Hin Kong and Sri Thanu, just north of Sonolito Thai Food. View on map ↗

Bubba's Roastery (Haad Yao)

Not just another branch — this is where Bubba's roasts its beans, so the coffee is the point. Expect a focused coffee experience alongside a solid brunch, in a relaxed Haad Yao setting. A good stop for anyone who wants to taste the roast at the source.

Why we love it — The roastery behind the beans, with coffee front and centre.
Best for — Specialty coffee · Coffee lovers · Brunch
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — In Haad Yao, on the west coast. View on map ↗

Balance Specialty Coffee

A specialty-coffee spot for people who take their morning cup seriously — carefully sourced beans, well-pulled espresso and an easy place to settle in. Popular with the Sri Thanu wellness and remote-work crowd.

Why we love it — Focused specialty coffee in the heart of the west coast.
Best for — Specialty coffee · Digital nomads · Quiet mornings
Price — ฿
Getting there — In the Sri Thanu area. View on map ↗

Chacha Coffee

Coffee is taken seriously here. Chacha pairs carefully sourced beans with well-pulled espresso and an easy atmosphere that keeps locals and remote workers coming back. Alongside espresso, filter and iced drinks, the all-day breakfast carries a subtle thread of Moroccan spice. Community events like the Friday Run Club have made it a favourite morning meeting spot.

Why we love it — Excellent specialty coffee in one of the island's friendliest café communities.
Best for — Specialty coffee · Breakfast · Digital nomads
Price — ฿
Getting there — On the west coast; open daily from 8 am. View on map ↗

Caffe Della Moca

Up in Chaloklum on the north coast, Caffe Della Moca brings an Italian-leaning approach to coffee — a reliable espresso stop in an area with fewer specialty options. Good for a proper cup before or after exploring the north.

Why we love it — A dependable Italian-style coffee stop in the north of the island.
Best for — Espresso · Coffee lovers · North coast mornings
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Chaloklum, on the north coast. View on map ↗

Healthy & Plant-Based Breakfast in Koh Phangan

Fresh ingredients, nourishing breakfasts and some of the island's best vegetarian and vegan cooking.

Deli Devi

Part deli, part artisan café, and unlike anywhere else on the island. Deli Devi specialises in handmade plant-based products — vegan cheeses, raw desserts, ceremonial cacao, homemade cakes, smoothies and specialty coffee. Drop in for a healthy breakfast, pick up ingredients or just have cake with a coffee.

Why we love it — Artisan plant-based products made with genuine attention to detail.
Best for — Healthy breakfast · Vegan treats · Coffee & cake
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — On the main road between Thong Sala and Chaloklum. View on map ↗

Eat.Co

A favourite for plant-based food that satisfies everyone, not only vegans. Everything is made from scratch with good ingredients — colourful bowls, hearty plates and a few creative recipes — in a warm, relaxed room that draws yogis, remote workers, travellers and residents alike.

Why we love it — Homemade vegan food with wide appeal, in one of Sri Thanu's warmest community spaces.
Best for — Vegan breakfast · Healthy lunch · Community
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Sri Thanu, at the heart of the wellness community. View on map ↗

ETHOS Wholefood Cafe & Shala

A wholefood café built around fresh, plant-forward cooking — nourishing breakfasts, bowls and wholefood plates in a calm Sri Thanu setting. A quiet favourite with the island's wellness community.

Why we love it — Fresh wholefood breakfasts in one of Sri Thanu's most easy-going rooms.
Best for — Wholefood breakfast · Vegan · Digital nomads
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Sri Thanu, on the west coast. View on map ↗

Kia Ora Café

A relaxed Sri Thanu café for a healthy, fresh start to the day — good breakfasts, bowls and coffee in an unhurried setting. An easy call after a morning class.

Why we love it — Fresh, healthy breakfasts in a relaxed Sri Thanu setting.
Best for — Healthy breakfast · Post-yoga · Coffee
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Sri Thanu, on the west coast. View on map ↗

Swiss Farmacy

Tucked into the greenery of Madua Wan, Swiss Farmacy is the kind of place that quickly feels like home. The focus is wholesome homemade cooking, organic ingredients and a genuinely relaxed pace — fresh sandwiches, changing daily specials and good coffee, with a few resident animals wandering about.

Why we love it — Wholesome homemade food in one of the island's most peaceful settings.
Best for — Healthy brunch · Homemade food · Families
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — In Madua Wan, surrounded by jungle in the heart of the island. View on map ↗

Seasalt & Sage

Fresh, wholesome food in a relaxed room — smoothie bowls, generous breakfasts, salads, good coffee and healthy brunch plates. Just walked the beach, or settling in with a laptop and a flat white, it's one of the coast's most relaxing cafés.

Why we love it — Relaxed brunches, good coffee and fresh, healthy dishes.
Best for — Healthy breakfast · Brunch · Specialty coffee
Price — ฿฿
Getting there — On the Ban Tai beachfront (also a Sri Thanu branch). View on map ↗

Bakeries & Artisan Pastries in Koh Phangan

Fresh bread, buttery croissants, artisan pastries and a good morning coffee to go with them.

German Bakery by Achim

A long-running name for proper bread and baked goods, German Bakery by Achim turns out fresh loaves, pretzels and pastries in the German tradition — a reliable stop for anyone missing a good crusty bread or a hearty bakery breakfast.

Why we love it — Proper German-style bread and pastries, baked fresh.
Best for — Fresh bread · Pastries · Bakery breakfast
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Thong Sala. View on map ↗

Nira's Home Bakery

A Thong Sala favourite for homemade cakes and pastries. Nira's has built a loyal following on baking made with care — a good stop for a sweet treat with coffee or something fresh to take away.

Why we love it — Homemade cakes and pastries made with genuine care.
Best for — Artisan pastries · Cakes · Coffee & cake
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Thong Sala, near the town centre. View on map ↗

French Bakery Phangan

For croissants and French-style bakes, French Bakery Phangan is the island's go-to — buttery viennoiserie, baguettes and pastries done in the French tradition, best early while everything's fresh from the oven.

Why we love it — French croissants and pastries, best straight from the oven.
Best for — Croissants · French pastries · Morning coffee
Price — ฿
Getting there — In Thong Sala. View on map ↗

A Perfect Morning in Koh Phangan

8:00 — coffee. Start with a specialty coffee at Indigo or Balance Specialty Coffee.

9:00 — breakfast. A generous brunch at Foxxy's Restaurant & Café, or a sea-view breakfast at SAGE in Ban Tai.

10:30 — yoga or beach. A late-morning yoga class, or time on the beach.

11:30 — something sweet. A fresh pastry from Nira's Home Bakery or French Bakery Phangan.

Final Thoughts

Few tropical islands do mornings quite like Koh Phangan. From specialty roasters and artisan bakeries to healthy bowls, vegan brunches and generous all-day breakfasts, the island rewards anyone who likes to start the day slowly and well.

Our advice is simple: don't settle on one café. Try a roaster one morning, a beachfront brunch the next, a bakery the day after. And when you're ready for lunch and dinner, our other local guides pick up where this one leaves off — the best restaurants in Koh Phangan, where to eat in Sri Thanu and where to eat in Hin Kong.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Where can I find the best breakfast in Koh Phangan?

Some of our favourites are Bubba's Coffee, Foxxy's Restaurant & Café, SAGE Breakfast & More and Mimi's Café, each doing generous breakfasts and brunch with good coffee. The island's best breakfast really depends on where you're staying — see our by-area picks below.

Which café serves the best specialty coffee in Koh Phangan?

For specialty coffee, Indigo Specialty Coffee & Bakery, Bubba's Roastery in Haad Yao, Balance Specialty Coffee and Chacha Coffee are among the island's best, with Caffe Della Moca a strong pick up in Chaloklum.

Where can I find vegan breakfast in Koh Phangan?

Sri Thanu is the island's plant-based hub. Eat.Co, ETHOS Wholefood Cafe & Shala and Deli Devi all do excellent vegan breakfasts, and most healthy cafés offer plant-based options.

What is the best brunch on the island?

For a relaxed brunch, Foxxy's Restaurant & Café, SAGE Breakfast & More and Bubba's Coffee are hard to beat — generous menus, good coffee and easy atmospheres.

Which bakery makes the best pastries in Koh Phangan?

For fresh bread and pastries, try German Bakery by Achim, Nira's Home Bakery in Thong Sala and French Bakery Phangan for croissants and French-style bakes.

Which cafés are good for digital nomads?

Foxxy's Restaurant & Café, Indigo, Balance Specialty Coffee, ETHOS Wholefood Cafe & Shala and Chacha Coffee are reliable for good coffee, Wi-Fi and a comfortable spot to work.

Where can I find breakfast near Sri Thanu?

Sri Thanu is packed with cafés — Foxxy's, Mimi's Café, Indigo, Deli Devi, Eat.Co and ETHOS Wholefood Cafe & Shala are all within a few minutes of each other.

Which cafés open early in Koh Phangan?

Many cafés open around 7–8 am for the post-yoga crowd, especially in Sri Thanu and Baan Tai. Chacha Coffee and the Bubba's cafés are dependable early starts; hours shift in low season, so check current times before you go.
