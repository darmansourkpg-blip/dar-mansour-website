The Design Collection

Bring a piece of Eden & Beyond into your home.

Limited-edition tables, lighting, transformed objects and dressed walls — collaged by hand, designed by the studio, available on their own. Discover, commission or acquire a single piece, no project required.

Every piece begins the way our projects do — with a story, a material, a wink. Some are born inside a commission and released as editions; others exist purely as objects. None are mass-produced.

Tables

Each table is a one-off — a familiar form dressed in paper, paint and layered collage until it reveals the invisible life it carries. Moroccan icons collided with Western art. Numbered, collectible, made to be lived with.
